Privacy Policy & Temrs Of Use

Baby&Life

Last updated: February 6, 2026

At Baby&Life, your family's privacy is our highest priority.
This Privacy Policy explains — in plain language — what information we collect (and what we don't) when you use the Baby&Life app, and how we handle it.

1. Information We Collect

Baby&Life is designed to work completely offline and keep every piece of data under your full control.

We collect and store only the information you choose to enter about your baby, such as:

  • Daily activities (meals, play time, sleep, potty, etc.)
  • Notes, enjoyment ratings, meal types, durations, and any custom text
  • Dates and times of your entries

All of this data stays exclusively on your device — it is never sent to any server, never uploaded, and never shared with anyone.

2. No Personal Identifiers

We do not collect:

  • Your name, email address, or phone number
  • Your location
  • Photos (unless you add them manually in future versions)
  • Any other personally identifiable information

There is no account creation, no login, and no tracking of any kind.

3. Technical & Analytics Data

We may receive very limited, fully anonymous technical information through Apple's standard App Analytics, such as:

  • Crash reports
  • Basic usage statistics (e.g., which features are used most)

This data never includes any personal details, baby information, or content from your logs.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use your data only to:

  • Show your baby's daily logs, charts, and summaries inside the app
  • Allow you to edit, review, or delete entries
  • Create simple visualizations (averages, progress over time)

That's it. We do not sell data. We do not share data. We do not use it for advertising or any other purpose.

5. Data Storage & Security

  • All your data is stored locally on your iOS device only.
  • We rely on Apple's strong built-in encryption and data protection features.
  • If you delete the app or erase its data, all your baby's logs are permanently removed from the device.

6. Your Rights & Control

You have complete control at all times:

  • Edit or delete any entry whenever you want
  • Remove all data instantly by deleting the app
  • No external account = nothing to delete elsewhere

7. Children Under 13

Baby&Life is not directed to children under 13. The app is designed exclusively for parents and guardians.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. If any significant change occurs, we will show a notice inside the app.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about privacy or your data, please feel free to reach out:

covieworganization.babylifeapp@gmail.com
